Vectis U14 Players' Player Vote

Vote for the Vectis U14 Players’ Player of the Season

It’s time to vote for the teammate you feel has best represented what it means to be part of this team.

You’ll be asked to choose your 1st, 2nd, and 3rd choices — and give a short reason for each. These should be the players you’re always glad to have on your side: those who show commitment, sportsmanship, and lead by example on and off the pitch.

As you consider your vote, think about the five core values of rugby:
Teamwork, Respect, Enjoyment, Discipline, and Sportsmanship.

Votes are named, so please take care and be thoughtful in your selections. Every vote counts!
